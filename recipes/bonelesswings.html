Boneless Wings

Perfrect for game day, these wings will take care of a party of 5 or more in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • Diced Chicken
  • Breading
  • Bottle of sauce, your choice

Steps

  1. Roll diced chicken in breading
  2. Spray baking sheet with oil
  3. Bake at 400degrees for 15 minutes
  4. Let rest for ten minutes, then toss in sauce.
  5. Serve!
