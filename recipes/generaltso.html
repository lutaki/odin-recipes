General Tso's Chicken

General Tso's Chicken is a easy to make, tangy, recipe that has been loved for hundreds of years.

Ingredients

  • Garlic Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken Breast
  • One Cup Uncooked Rice
  • Corn Starch

Steps

  1. Mix the garlic sauce, soy sauce in a bowl.
  2. Dice the chicken breast into pieces that are approximately 1 inch by 1 inch.
  3. Lightly coat the chicken breast in Corn starch.
  4. Add one tbsp of oil into a pan and cook the starched chicken on medium heat until golden brown.
  5. Drain the oil from the pan, add the sauce mixture, and coat the chicken evenly until all pieces are covered.
    6. \
  6. Serve over white rice and enjoy.
