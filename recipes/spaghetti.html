Spaghetti and Meatballs

The classic italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Box of Spaghetti noodles
  • Prego Red Sauce
  • 1lb of Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Boil water, add noodles and cook until preferred chewiness.
  2. Drain water, add sauce to pan
  3. Form beef into balls, cook with oil on stove until fully cooked through.
  4. Add meatballs to pot with sauce and noodles, mix until combined fully.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
